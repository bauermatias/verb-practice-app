📓 B33P's Notebook

Affirmative ✅ — I / You / We / They + verb. He / She / It + verb -s. Ej: She watches TV. They watch TV.
Negative 🚫 — I/You/We/They + don't + verb. He/She/It + doesn't + verb. Ej: I don't like coffee. She doesn't like coffee.
Questions ❓ — Do + I/you/we/they + verb...? Does + he/she/it + verb...? Ej: Do you play tennis? Does she play tennis?
Adverbs of frequency ⏰ — go before the main verb, but after am/is/are. Ej: She always plays. She is always happy.
